About Me

ello! My name is Peter Shamoun, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Data Science at the University of California, San Diego, with an expected graduation in June 2027. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in data structures, statistical modeling, and the theoretical underpinnings of data science, which I continue to build upon through hands-on research and practical experiences. I am deeply passionate about harnessing data to solve complex, real-world problems. As an undergraduate researcher at the Data Science Alliance, I am leading a project focused on optimizing food bank locations across San Diego using a combination of QGIS, Python, SQL, and machine learning models. This project aims to provide actionable insights for policymakers, ensuring more efficient food distribution to underserved communities. My professional background includes a data analyst internship at Nexon America, where I developed intricate SQL queries across multiple data sources to uncover key user behavior metrics and created Tableau dashboards that empowered the marketing team to make data-driven decisions. The impact of my work was recognized through a company-wide feature. Additionally, during my time as a research assistant at the San Diego Supercomputer Center, I contributed to the creation of a large-scale database and developed a neural network model with 95% accuracy in predicting matrix compression errors. Beyond research and internships, I have also contributed as a software engineering intern at STEMz Learning, where I enhanced a recommendation system's performance and improved user authentication processes, achieving significant efficiency gains. I enjoy working at the intersection of technology and data analytics, constantly seeking opportunities to learn, adapt, and make meaningful contributions. My technical toolkit includes Python, SQL, Java, R, MATLAB, JavaScript, and experience with tools like Snowflake, AWS, Tableau, and various machine learning libraries. When I'm not coding or analyzing data, I'm often working on personal projects, like my AI-powered supermarket deal finder app, which combines web scraping, computer vision, and user-friendly interfaces to deliver practical value. I'm excited to share my journey, projects, and insights here on my website. Feel free to reach out or connect with me for collaboration or professional opportunities. Let's leverage data to create impactful solutions together!
